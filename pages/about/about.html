How to use the Quantifitness Calculator

Step 1

Select from the drop down menu the activity you are planning to do.

Step 2

Input your weight in Kilograms.

Step 3

Input how long you plan to do the activity in minutes.

Step 4

Watch as our calculator does its magic and presents your calories burned and weight loss!
